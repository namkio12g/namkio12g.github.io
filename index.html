Inspired (Youtube-Clone)

This project is a full-stack, microservices-based video streaming platform inspired by YouTube. Users can log in securely via Google OAuth2, and sessions are managed with JWT to ensure secure, token-based access. Key features include video uploading, storage, and playback, with media files and their thumbnails processed through FFmpeg to generate multiple resolutions for optimal streaming quality across devices.
